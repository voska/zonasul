zonasul

Order groceries from Zona Sul supermarket in Rio de Janeiro, from your terminal.

$ zonasul search "leite"
1   Leite Integral Itambe 1L      R$8.79
2   Leite Desnatado Parmalat     R$7.49
3   Leite Condensado Moca        R$9.29
$ zonasul cart add 12345 --qty 2
Added to cart.
